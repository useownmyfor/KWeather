# KWeather

KWeather is a lightweight, privacy-first weather app for South Korea.

Powered directly by official open data from the Korea Meteorological Administration (KMA), KWeather provides accurate weather forecasts without requiring an account or tracking your location.

## Features

* 🇰🇷 Official weather forecasts from the Korea Meteorological Administration (KMA)
* 🌤 Smart daily schedule with detailed short-term forecasts
* 🌡 Compare today's weather with yesterday's high, low, and average temperatures
* 📅 Official 3- to 10-day weather outlook
* 🔒 Privacy first — no login required and no live GPS tracking

## Weather Data

KWeather uses official weather data provided through the Korea Meteorological Administration (KMA) Open API.

KWeather is an independent application and is not affiliated with or endorsed by the Korea Meteorological Administration.

## Privacy

KWeather does not collect, store, or share personal information.
