## Frequently Asked Questions

**Where does KWeather get its weather data?**

KWeather uses official weather data provided through the Korea Meteorological Administration (KMA) Open API.

**Does KWeather track my location?**

No. KWeather does not collect or track your live GPS location. You simply select your city or weather station from the built-in offline database.
