## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Where does KWeather get its weather data?**<br>A: KWeather uses official weather data provided through the Korea Meteorological Administration (KMA) Open API.


**Q: Does KWeather track my location?**<br>A: No. KWeather does not collect or track your live GPS location. You simply select your city from the built-in offline database.
