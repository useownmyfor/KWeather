# KWeather Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how **KWeather** handles information when you use the app.

## 1. Information We Collect

KWeather does **not** collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require user accounts, email registration, or any personally identifiable information.

## 2. Location Data

KWeather does **not** access, collect, or track your device's live GPS location.

Instead, you can select your preferred city from the app's built-in offline database to view weather forecasts.

## 3. Weather Data

KWeather retrieves official weather information through the Korea Meteorological Administration (KMA) Open API.

No personal information is transmitted to the Korea Meteorological Administration (KMA) or any other third party when retrieving weather data.

## 4. Third-Party Services

The app fetches public weather forecast data directly from the Korea Meteorological Administration (KMA) open APIs. No personal data is sent to the KMA or any other third-party servers.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the Effective Date above will be updated accordingly.
